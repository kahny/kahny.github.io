about  me

After graduating from Cal and working in the finance/accounting area for about three years, I’ve become more and more certain that I am ready for a career change. Working with a wide variety of departments within my current company, and just even talking to my friends has made me realize the importance of aiming for a job where you find the challenges most rewarding and the work most interesting. Although I’m still just beginning, I believe my interest in these courses along with talking to my friends at work who are web developers has made me want to become a developer myself. I want to be able to create something useful from scratch, use more problem solving skills, and see the results of my work.
